Privacy Policy for Speaker Cleaner

Last updated: March 15, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the mobile application Speaker Cleaner ("the App") handles user data.

We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about our data practices.

Speaker Cleaner does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The App processes all functionality directly on the user's device.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

Speaker Cleaner does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, address, etc.)
  • Usage data or analytics
  • Device identifiers
  • Location data
  • Photos, files, or media
  • Microphone or audio recordings
  • Contact information
  • Health-related data

No data is sent to external servers, cloud services, or third parties.

3. How the App Works

The App generates audio frequencies and uses vibrations to help remove moisture or debris from the device speaker.

All processing takes place locally on the device.

No information about the user or the performance of the App is collected or stored.

4. Purchases and Subscriptions

Speaker Cleaner offers two types of auto-renewable subscriptions:

  • Weekly subscription
  • Lifetime access

Subscriptions unlock all features of the App.

The App is available for free for the first 3 days. After the trial period, a subscription is required to continue using the full functionality.

All payments and subscription management are handled securely through the Apple App Store.

We do not have access to your payment information.

You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your Apple ID settings.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not use:

  • Analytics tools
  • Advertising SDKs
  • Tracking technologies
  • Third-party integrations

No data is shared with any external services or partners.

6. Children's Privacy

Speaker Cleaner does not include age-restricted content and is suitable for all ages.

The App does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Security

Since the App does not collect or store any user data, there are no risks related to data theft or unauthorized access.

All App features operate entirely offline.

8. Disclaimer

Speaker Cleaner is not a medical product.

It does not diagnose, prevent, or treat any medical conditions.

The App is a utility tool intended only for cleaning device speakers using sound and vibration techniques.

We do not guarantee specific outcomes, as effectiveness may vary by device.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
